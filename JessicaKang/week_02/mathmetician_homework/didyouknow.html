IN ADDITION...

Einstein referred to him as a Denktier, a think animal.
It was a common occurrence for him to begin scribbling with pencil and paper in the midst of a nightclub or a lively party.
On his deathbed, Von Neumann entertained his brother by reciting from heart, word-for-word the first few lines of each page of Goethe's Faust.
Pop culture reference: "Mathematics and The Hunger Games" by Michael A Lewis.
