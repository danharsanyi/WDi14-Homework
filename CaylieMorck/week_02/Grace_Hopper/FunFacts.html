Grace Hopper was known for contributing to Maths and Sciences in many ways, and was acknowledged as the genius she was in many ways. Below is a short-list of the awards she was given both during her lifetime and posthumesly.



1969: Computer Sciences Man of Year Award
1971: Grace Murray Hopper Award for Outstanding Young Computer Professionals was established.
1973: First American and first woman to be made a Distinguished Fellow of the British Computer Society.
1982: American Association of University Women Achievement Award
1986: She recieved the Defense Distinguished Service Medal upon her retirement.
1991: National Medal of Technology.
1991: Elected a Fellow of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences.
1996: USS Hopper was launched and nicknamed Amazing Grace, it is on a very short list of US military vessels named after women.
